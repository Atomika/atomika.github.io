About

London and UK-based senior compositor and visual effects artist with 15 years of experience in feature films, television, and commercials. I've had the opportunity to work at some of the industry's top post-production houses, including Framestore, Double Negative, Industrial Light & Magic, and The Mill.


Over the years, I've developed a broad skill set in compositing, tackling a wide range of challenges, with a particular passion for high-end CG compositing. You can see some of the projects I've been fortunate to be part of here: Work.


If you'd like to connect — whether for opportunities, inquiries, or just to say hello — feel free to reach out.
